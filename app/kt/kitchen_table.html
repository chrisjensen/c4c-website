Making climate change a social affair

Introduction ...

Congratulations and a huge thanks for signing up to Climate for Change’s Kitchen Table Conversations program.

You are amongst the first to try out this program. So make your way through it with a sense of curiosity and adventure.

Save the date: Have you set the day, time and place for your first Kitchen Table Conversation? If not: contact your friends now to arrange it.

 

Each time your group meets you will:

Get Informed - Drill into what climate change means, the solutions and explore questions together

Reach Out - Learn and practice techniques for having effective climate conversations

Stand Up - Push on one of the levers of power to take action on climate change

Each conversation includes:

  • Get Ready: Some reading to get you ready for for your next . Time poor? Give it your best go, it should take you no longer than half an hour. Getting ready includes:

    • The climate change story: each week you’ll get the next part of the story about climate change and how we solve it.

    • Better conversations: An introduction to new ideas about having better climate conversations, which you will discuss when you meet up.

 

  • The Conversation: Follow through your Conversation Plan to...

    • Get Informed: Learn about and discuss special topics on the ins and outs of climate change and climate action

    • Reach Out: A Conversation Tutorial and Activity to give you the confidence to have fulfilling and meaningful conversations about climate change

    • Stand Up: We’ll set you a challenge to act on climate change and give you all the instructions you’ll need to achieve it.

 

  • The Homework:Homework!? We promise it's not like what you did (or didn’t do) in school.

    • Conversations: Have a go at practicing what you’ve learnt in your Conversation Tutorial

    • Challenges: With your latest set of instructions in hand, go to it! Need help? Get in touch.

    • Get Ready for your next Kitchen Table Conversation

    • Tell your friends about what you’re up to - no good keeping it a secret.

This may seem like a lot, but we’ll step you through it and we’re just an email away if you need a hand.

Nervous? That’s completely fine. We’re here for you, so are your friends, and many more people and organisations as you go on you'll discover as you go on; your partners in creating a great future for all of us.
